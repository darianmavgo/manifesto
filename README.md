# Darian Hickman Manifesto Repository

This directory contains drafts, notes, and the core conceptual framework for the "Darian Hickman Manifesto" and the "Future Proofing Government" writing samples. The collective objective of these documents is to outline a modern, data-driven, and highly resilient approach to governance.

## Existing Documents Summary

*   [**Numerica.md**](./Numerica.md)
    A concise markdown document outlining the fundamental tenets of "Darian’s Distributed World Order." Key concepts include transforming government into human-readable operations (akin to code), implementing a "Data Driven Distributed Democracy" where laws and entities have a maximum lifespan of 100 years, and eliminating misaligned, results-free administrative programs.
*   [**Hickman Manifesto.pdf**](./Hickman%20Manifesto.pdf)
    A draft document outlining top problems in current governments, with a focus on freedom of speech and establishing global rights. It lists influential economic and political texts as foundational research and proposes concepts like "Global Copy Paste 1.0" for standardizing successful laws across borders, alongside mandates for self-reliance.
*   [**Notes for Writing Sample Future Proofing Government.pdf**](./Notes%20for%20Writing%20Sample%20Future%20Proofing%20Government.pdf)
    A comprehensive outline exploring reforms across multiple sectors including elections, healthcare, and trade. Highlights include creating a "ScoreCard" to measure a government's success based on how many people choose to live and invest there, expanding election inclusion (e.g., online voting, felon enfranchisement), and utilizing free-market mechanisms before government mandates.
